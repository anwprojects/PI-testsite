About Our Company

As a proprietorship firm established in 1989, Pratik Industries manufactures and distributes a wide variety of industrial ventilation & exhaust systems as well as pollution control systems, including air handling blowers, exhaust blowers, centrifugal blowers, etc. Besides being a domestic supplier, we also engage in international supply through a third party.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing quality products and services, maximizing customer satisfaction and encouraging innovation as part of our mission.

Our Vision

To be the leading industry in our products and services while providing reliable service and installation. Also, lead the way toward more efficient and effective methods and procedures.

Why Choose Us

We provide turnkey solution right from the concept to commercialization in the field of spray sys HVAC sys & air pollution control sys. based on the requirement of end user in the compliance with US FDA 21 CFR Part 11, WHO-GMP, USFDA, MHRA, TGA and cGMP.

Who We Are

One of the leading manufacturers under ssi sector, we are offering a range of systems that meet the requirements of pharmaceutical, cement, glass, power and sugar industries.we make use of quality raw material like mild steel, stainless steel, aluminum, pp/frp or frp for designing our industrial ventilation & exhaust systems and pollution control systems.
