Pollution Control System (Scrubbing System)

We are Offering our clients with venture pollution control equipment Scrubbing systems that are used for effective gas cleaning even with wide flow variation with minimum pressure drop. Delivering desired performance with effectiveness, these systems are reckined for the following benefits.

  • High collection efficiency of 97% & above.
  • Maintenance free as designed is stationary part.
  • No evaporative liquid loss.
  • Adjustable throat to suit specific application.
  • Available in MS, S.S & FRP Material.
  • Capacity from 500 MCH to 60000 MCH.
  • These systems are operated in the following manner.
  • Dust laden gas enters the system through gas inlet.
  • Scrubbing liquid is introduced on the wall through spilt jet nozzle.
  • Gas & liquid are thoroughly mixed in the venture throat.
  • Gas with entrained solids and liquid enters separator
  • Liquid flows downward with solid and dissolved gas to slurry setting tank
  • Cleaned gases are exhausted to atmosphere through a top gas outlet

We are also offering our clients with packed bed scrubbing that find extensive application in chemical processing applications for gas and odor absorption and fume control.

Pollution Control System (Dust Collector System)

Our dust collector systems are equipped with the following components & pants:

  • Modified drop out section to operate the system continuously with high efficiency at low dust load on filter bag.
  • Cylindrical filter bag / cartridge filter with wire cage assembled from plenum, chamber for easy servicing & maintenance.
  • Can be provided with motorized manual, piles jet or reverse jet type bag cleaning arrangement.
  • Sequence control timer of suitable range.
  • Filter bag media & cages as per type, nature & conditions of air material to be separated & passed through.

These dust collector systems are operated through the following principle:

  • Dust laden air enters the unit at high conveying velocity.
  • The air is directed to the drop out section provided to reduce dust load filter media.
  • Typical baffle plate reduced the velocity of coarser particle & allows fall down in collecting hopper.
  • This arrangement reduces about 80% of dust load on filter media.
  • The deposits outside of the filter bag are cleaned though bursts of metered compresses air, injected venture tubes.
  • Control of intensity & volume if air is done by quick action, pilot operated diaphragm valves.
  • This results in an ideal cleaning action (Clean plant air at 6-8 Kg/Cm is adequate in case of pulse jet).
  • Special attention is given to the type, quantity of dust material to ensures superior filtration & performance.
